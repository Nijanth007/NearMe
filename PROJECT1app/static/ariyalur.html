Amman Temple

Amman Temple is an ancient and revered Hindu temple located in Ariyalur, Chennai. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple holds significant religious and cultural importance in the area. The main deity, known as Dhandeeswarar, represents Lord Shiva in the form of a "Linga," with the temple also housing a shrine for Goddess Dharmasamvardhini, a form of Parvati.
